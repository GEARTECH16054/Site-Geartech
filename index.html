Our Team

The Geartech Canaã team started its work in November 2017, and so far we have won the titles of Triple Brazilian Champion and World Champion in the Asian Pacific World Champion Tournament, in the Teams FTC reached category.

  • 2018/2019

    Rover Ruckus

    In 2018 we won our first National Inspire Awards, in the Rover Ruckus season. It was a surprise for all of us as it was our first year and everyone was new to the tournament. Thanks to this achievement, we traveled to the World Championship - Teams FTC reached, in Houston. Where we brought home the prize...

  • 2019/2020

    Skystone

    In the 2019/2020 season, our team again won the National "Inspire Awards", with that we managed to qualify for the World Championship - Teams FTC reached again. But unfortunately, the COVID-19 pandemic occurred and the World tournament was cancelled.

  • 2020/2021

    Ultimate Goal

    Amid the pandemic, our team outdid itself in all aspects, going beyond any challenge that the pandemic put us. So we won the National Championship, winning the "Inspire Awards " for the third time in a row and qualified for the Asia Open Pacific Championship, the only World (online) tournament in the midst of the pandemic. And so we won the World "Inspire Awards ", 3rd Place in the Engineering Notebook and Alliance Finalist. This season we brought about 11 titles to the SESI Canaã school only by Teams FTC reached.

Teams Progression

In our school we have our team progression within the FIRST tournaments, where members move up in teams according to their age. It all starts at FLL, FTC, and finally FRC. And then many of our members become mentors and even local teams.

  • First Lego League

    Geek SESI Canaã

    The SESI Canaã Geek team starts our entire progression process, since all members are from Elementary School I (1st to 5th grade). Thus they have their first contact with the community and FIRST competitions.

  • First Lego League

    Life SESI Canaã

    Life SESI Canaã formed in 2018, is one of the bases for the FTC and FRC teams, and is made up of members from Elementary II (6th to 9th grade). The team in its history has already won about 8 trophies in Regional and National tournaments, besides that in the City Shaper season achieved a spot for the World Festival, but they were not because of the COVID-19 pandemic.

  • First Lego League

    Gametech Canaã

    Formed in 2014 the Gametech Canaan team, along with Life, also forms the basis of our FTC and FRC teams. Thus formed by high school members, with members who have or have not participated in FIRST. The team in its history has about 16 trophies, being of Regional, National and International. The team in the Into Orbit season brought home the 1st Place Champions Award - West Virginia.

  • Geartech Canaã

    First Tech Challenge

    The Geartech Canaan team is the technical base for FIRST tournaments at our school. On the team students already work with industrial programming (Java), work with sponsors and large events, and advanced engineering. The team consists of members from our FLL teams (Life and Gametech) that just finished their season. They also mentor teams starting out in the First Lego League.

  • Infinity BR

    First Robotics Competition

    And finishing our team progression, we have the Infinity BR team that works at the highest level of FIRST tournaments both in Engineering, and Event Organization. The members are all from the FTC team (Geartech Canaã), being very experienced members in both National and International tournaments. The team in its first participation in Off-Season Brazil, won the Rookie All-Star award. Besides always its members continue in the world FIRST as mentors, judges and even coaches.
